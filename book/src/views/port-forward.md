# Port Forward

Press `p` on a Pod or Service row to open the port-forward dialog. Manage active forwards with `Ctrl+Shift+P`.

---

## Keybindings

### Port Forwards Panel

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| `j` / `Down` | Next forward |
| `k` / `Up` | Previous forward |
